# Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage
<h2>Firebase Realtime Database and Storage application:</h2>
This is a Simple Note Making Application, It uses Firebase Realtime Database and storage.
<br><h3>Screenshots:</h3></br>
<img width="1280" alt="WhatsApp Image 2023-12-03 at 12 36 03 AM" src="https://github.com/Sajidkkhan/Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage/assets/11984074/a69d0a94-3007-424f-bdad-bc04c46cdd48">

<br><h3>How to Save Data to the Firebase Realtime Database in Android?</br></h3>
<br>Step 1: Create a New Project in Android Studio.</br>
<br>Step 2: Connect your app to Firebase: Navigate to the Tools option on the top bar. Inside that click on Firebase. </br>

<img width="1280" alt="Screen Shot 2023-12-03 at 12 03 42 AM" src="https://github.com/Sajidkkhan/Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage/assets/11984074/29e70953-8ffe-46c7-8bc4-fb424ea64526">
<br>Step 3: At the right side, you will see a window, Navigate to the Firebase Realtime Database.</br>

<img width="1280" alt="Screen Shot 2023-12-03 at 12 07 39 AM" src="https://github.com/Sajidkkhan/Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage/assets/11984074/206f7a3f-57cd-4601-97f0-12d01146cc60">

<br>Step 4: Click on "Connect to Firebase". </br>
<br>Step 5: Click on "Add the Realtime Database to your App". </br>

![Realtimedbsetting](https://github.com/Sajidkkhan/Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage/assets/11984074/5c38c2f8-caef-458f-8798-c0cb78f557ca)
<br>Step 6: Wait for sometime, Your project will be connected to Firebase and Realtime database.</br>

<img width="1280" alt="Screen Shot 2023-12-03 at 12 21 53 AM" src="https://github.com/Sajidkkhan/Note-making-Application-Using-Firebase-Realtime-Database-and-storage/assets/11984074/49f22f1b-0612-4e86-9a60-017c0efea83f">

<br>Step 7: Add permissions:</br>
      <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"/>
      <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE"/>
<br>Step 8: And Your project is ready.</br>
